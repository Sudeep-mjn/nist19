Top Questions

How do I open an account?

To open an account, visit our Online Account Opening page and follow the instructions.

What are the trading hours?

Our trading hours are Sunday to Friday, 10:00 AM to 5:00 PM.

KYC

What is KYC?

KYC stands for Know Your Customer. It is a process of verifying the identity of our clients.

How do I submit my KYC documents?

You can submit your KYC documents through our online portal or visit our office to submit them in person.

Settlement

How long does settlement take?

Settlement usually takes 2-3 business days.

What documents are required for settlement?

You will need to provide your trade confirmation, ID proof, and address proof.

Commission Structure

Transparent and competitive commission rates for all your trading needs.

Instrument Commission Rate Minimum Commission Example
Equity 0.40% of transaction amount Rs. 25 For Rs. 50,000 trade: Rs. 200
Government Bonds 0.10% of transaction amount Rs. 50 For Rs. 1,00,000 trade: Rs. 100
Corporate Bonds & Debentures 0.20% of transaction amount Rs. 50 For Rs. 75,000 trade: Rs. 150
Close-End Bonds 0.30% of transaction amount Rs. 50 For Rs. 60,000 trade: Rs. 180

Additional Fees

  • DP Fee: Rs. 25 per script (charged by CDSC)
  • SEBON Fee: 0.015% of transaction amount (applicable on equity only)
  • Capital Gain Tax: As per government regulations
